Surveillance Camera System

Consulting, Design, and Implementation

As a company specializing in providing consulting, design, and implementation services for security and surveillance systems, we develop and apply a set of standards for designing surveillance camera systems to ensure efficiency, stability, and safety for businesses in all areas of operation.

No Blind Spots

Strategic placement ensures full observation of key areas, tailored to environmental conditions.

AI & Modern Tech

High-resolution IP cameras featuring facial recognition, intrusion detection, and people counting.

Stable & Secure

Independent internal networks and VLANs to prevent unauthorized access and protect data.

1. Tailored to Actual Needs

Each business has a different operating model and security requirements. A thorough on-site survey and security risk assessment are essential for developing the optimal surveillance solution.

2. Ensuring Coverage and Image Quality

  • The camera system is arranged according to the principle of no blind spots, ensuring full observation of key areas such as entrances, warehouses, production areas, and offices.
  • Resolution, viewing angle, night vision capabilities, and environmental conditions are always calculated accordingly to ensure crystal-clear footage at all times.

3. Integration of Modern Technology

We apply advanced technologies to bring your security infrastructure into the modern era, including:

  • High-resolution IP cameras.
  • Recording with H.265+/H.264+ compression to save bandwidth and storage space.
  • RAID storage systems and secure Cloud Backup integration.
  • AI integration: Advanced features such as facial recognition, active intrusion detection, and automated people counting.

4. System Stability and Security

  • Design the system using an independent internal network or separate VLANs to ensure operational stability and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Implement strict security standards including access control, data encryption, and hierarchical storage policies.

5. Scalability and Long-Term Maintenance

The system is designed for flexible scalability, allowing for easy upgrades or the addition of new cameras as your business grows. Furthermore, comprehensive after-sales service, maintenance, and operational monitoring are natively integrated into our overall solution.
